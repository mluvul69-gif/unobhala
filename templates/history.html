History & Overview

placeWhere and What the School Is Today

Unobhala High School (sometimes called Unobhala Secondary School) is a public secondary school in Enkanyezini, KwaZulu‑Natal (KZN), South Africa. It is located under the KwaNyavu Tribal Authority in the rural uMkhambathini Municipality.

It serves learners from Grade 8 to Grade 12 — typical of South African secondary education. It is classified as a Quintile 3 non‑fee paying school. EMIS number: 500289969.
