Our Mission

Unobhala Secondary School is committed to delivering high-quality education that empowers learners with knowledge, critical thinking skills, and strong moral values. We strive to create a safe, inclusive, and disciplined learning environment where every learner is encouraged to reach their full academic and personal potential.

Our mission is to prepare learners not only for examinations, but for life — developing confident, responsible, and respectful young citizens who can positively contribute to their communities.

Our Core Values

The school promotes a culture built on respect, accountability, integrity, perseverance, and excellence. We believe discipline and consistency are the foundation of academic success, while teamwork and community involvement strengthen leadership and character development.

Curriculum Offered

Unobhala Secondary School follows the national curriculum prescribed by the South African Department of Basic Education. The academic programme is designed to equip learners with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills required for higher education and career development.

Subjects offered include Mathematics, Mathematical Literacy, English, IsiZulu, Life Orientation, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Geography, History, Accounting, Business Studies, and Economics. The school also encourages participation in academic enrichment and leadership programmes.

School Phases

The school accommodates learners from the Senior Phase (Grades 8–9) through to the Further Education and Training Phase (Grades 10–12). During these critical years, learners are prepared for the National Senior Certificate examinations while being guided in career awareness and subject specialization.

Principal’s Message

Welcome to Unobhala Secondary School. Our vision is to cultivate a culture of excellence where every learner is supported, challenged, and inspired to succeed. We believe education is a partnership between the school, parents, and the community.

Through dedication, discipline, and strong leadership, we aim to produce well-rounded learners who are academically prepared, socially responsible, and confident in their future paths. We encourage our learners to dream big, work hard, and uphold the values of respect and integrity in everything they do.
