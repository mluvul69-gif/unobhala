Who We Are

Unobhala Secondary School is committed to academic excellence, personal development, and community responsibility. We provide a supportive learning environment that prepares learners for success in an ever-changing world.

To educate and inspire learners through quality teaching, discipline, and values that promote lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be a leading secondary school that develops responsible, skilled, and confident individuals for the future.

Our Responsibility

We believe in integrity, respect, accountability, and excellence. Our responsibility extends beyond academics to shaping disciplined, socially aware, and motivated learners.
