Welcome Future Learners

We accept learners from Grade 8 to Grade 12. A once-off R150 Online Application Fee is required before submitting the application form.

Admission Requirements

  • Certified copy of learner's birth certificate
  • Latest school report
  • Transfer letter (if applicable)
  • Copy of parent/guardian ID
  • Proof of residence

Application Process

  1. Pay the R150 online application fee
  2. Complete the online application form
  3. Upload required documents
  4. Application review by administration
  5. Receive confirmation and placement
